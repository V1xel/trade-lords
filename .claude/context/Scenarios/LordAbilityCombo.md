# Lord Ability Interactions

## Scenario 1: Bertram Factory Spam

**Setup**: Sir Bertram, Round 2, £20 gold

**Turn 1**: Build Cheese Dairy on £3 grassland
- Cost: £6 - £3 (1st factory discount) = £3
- Total: £3 + £3 = £6
- Remaining: £14

**Turn 2**: Build Bakery on £2 grassland
- Cost: £6 - £3 (1st bakery discount) = £3
- Total: £3 + £2 = £5
- Remaining: £9

**Turn 3**: Build 2nd Cheese Dairy on £3 grassland
- Cost: £6 - £4 (2nd dairy discount) = £2
- Total: £2 + £3 = £5
- Remaining: £4

**Result**: 3 factories for £16 (vs standard £27)

## Scenario 2: Aldric Dual Contracts

**Setup**: Lord Aldric, Round 1

**Turn 1**: Take 2 Contracts action
- Contract A: "3 Wool → £12 + 3 VP"
- Contract B: "2 Milk + 1 Cheese → £15 + 1 Cotton + 4 VP"
- Cost: £4 (pay once for both)
- Result: 2 unfulfilled contracts

**Rounds 2-3**: Build production for both
- Sheep for Wool
- Cows + Dairy for Milk/Cheese

**Round 4**: Fulfill both contracts
- Deliver goods for Contract A
- Deliver goods for Contract B
- Total: £27 + 1 Cotton + 7 VP

## Scenario 3: Elspeth Milk Economy

**Setup**: Dame Elspeth, 3 Cows

**Production Phase**: 3 Cows → 3 Milk

**Action Phase**: Sell Milk to Elspeth
- Sell 3 Milk for £24 (3 × £8)
- Uses 0 merchants
- Market unaffected

**Next Turn**: Trade action available
- Sell other resources on market
- Merchants still free

**Efficiency**: £24 income + merchants available for other trades
