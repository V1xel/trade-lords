# Complete Round Flow

## Initial State
**Round 2, Player: Lady Isolde**
- Gold: £20
- Resources: 2 Wool, 1 Milk
- Units: 2 Sheep, 1 Cow, 1 Cheese Dairy
- Merchants: 5 available
- Contracts: None

## Action Phase

**Turn 1 - Take Contract**:
- Take "3 Wool + 1 Cheese → £18 + 4 VP"
- Pay £5 (Round 2 cost)
- State: £15, contract unfulfilled

**Turn 2 - Expand**:
- Place 1 Sheep on £2 grassland
- Pay £5 + £2 = £7
- State: £8, 3 Sheep total

**Turn 3 - Trade**:
- Sell 2 Wool at £6 each = £12
- Isolde bonus: +£1
- Use 1 merchant
- State: £21, 0 Wool, 4 merchants

**Turn 4 - Pass**:
- Receive passing bonus: £3
- State: £24

## Production Phase

**Production**:
- 3 Sheep → 3 Wool
- 1 Cow → 1 Milk
- Total: 3 Wool, 2 Milk

**Processing**:
- Cheese Dairy: 1 Milk → 1 Cheese
- State: 3 Wool, 1 Milk, 1 Cheese

**Result**: Can now fulfill contract (3 Wool + 1 Cheese)

## Scoring Phase

Round 2 tile: "Most Wool in stock"
- Isolde has 3 Wool (after production, before fulfill)
- Wins 1st place: +3 glory

## Round End

- Merchants refresh: 5 available
- State: £24, 3 Wool, 1 Milk, 1 Cheese, contract ready
- Next round: Can fulfill contract immediately
